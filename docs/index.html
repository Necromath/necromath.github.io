Mauricio Montes

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from the University of Texas at Austin in 2021 (Hook ’em) and am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. student in Mathematics at Auburn University.

I am currently based in Paris, where I am visiting Sorbonne Université through May 2026 and working with Grégoire Sergeant-Perthuis on questions at the intersection of chemical reaction networks, probabilistic graphical models, and geometry.

My research lies broadly in applied and algebraic topology, category theory, probability, and algebraic geometry, with a current emphasis on chemical reaction networks and related structures arising in inference and dynamics.

You can read more about my research here.

I also help organize the Directed Reading Program at Auburn.
